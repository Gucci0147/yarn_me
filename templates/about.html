About Us

Welcome to YARN_ME.NG! I'm Al-Jannatul Fridaous, a passionate 24 year old crochet designer and enthusiast dedicated to creating cozy,
colorful, and unique pieces. With a passion for connecting with fellow crochet lovers, I'm excited to share my expertise and collaborate
with clients who share my enthusiasm for yarn.

As a skilled crochet artist, I offer custom services, including bespoke dresses, hoodies, and more. My goal is to bring your crochet dreams to life with attention to detail, quality craftsmanship, and a friendly approach. Let's work together to create something truly special!

Feel free to explore my portfolio, learn more about my services, and let's get started on your next crochet project!
